🔄 Prefix — Buy New, Sell Old

A circular e-commerce platform where you can sell your pre-owned products and discover new ones — all in one place.

🛍️ About Prefix

Prefix is a dual-purpose e-commerce marketplace that bridges the gap between second-hand sustainability and fresh retail. Whether you want to give your old belongings a new life or find your next favorite product — Prefix is your go-to platform.
Our mission is simple: reduce waste, save money, and make buying and selling effortless.

Platform-Wide

🔑 User Authentication — Sign up / log in via email or social accounts (Google, Facebook)
📱 Mobile Responsive — Fully optimized for web and mobile browsers
🌐 Multi-Category Support — Electronics, clothing, furniture, books, and more
🌿 Sustainability Score — Each second-hand purchase earns eco-impact points
